Lapskaus (Norwegian Stew)

Image source: https://www.pinterest.com/pin/best-traditional-beef-stew-recipe-foodcom

Description: A comforting stew made with meat and root vegetables, perfect for cold days.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g (1 lb) beef or lamb, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 leek, sliced
  • 1 liter (4 cups) beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the meat in a little oil.
  2. Add onions and cook until soft.
  3. Add the broth, carrots, potatoes, and leek.
  4. Simmer for about 1.5 hours until the meat is tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
