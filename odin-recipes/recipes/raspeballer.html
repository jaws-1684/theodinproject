Raspeballer (Potato Dumplings)

Image source: https://scandinaviancookbook.com

Description: Raspeballer are hearty potato dumplings often served with meat and gravy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg (2.2 lbs) potatoes
  • 200 g (1.5 cups) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 200 g (7 oz) meat (beef or pork, optional)
  • Butter (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the potatoes until tender. Drain and let them cool slightly.
  2. Peel the potatoes and mash them while still warm.
  3. Mix in flour, salt, and egg (if using) to form a dough.
  4. Divide the dough into balls. If using meat, place a small piece inside each ball.
  5. Boil the dumplings in salted water for about 15-20 minutes until they float to the surface.
  6. Serve with melted butter or gravy.
