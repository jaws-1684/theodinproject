Kjøttkaker (Meatballs)

Image source: https://middaymat.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/kjottkaker.jpg?w=650

Description: Kjøttkaker are traditional Norwegian meatballs, often served with potatoes and brown gravy.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • Butter (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together all the ingredients until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into round meatballs.
  3. Heat butter in a frying pan over medium heat and fry the meatballs until browned and cooked through.
  4. Serve with boiled potatoes, gravy, and lingonberry sauce.
